About Me

Hello, I'm Olivia Hanks, a passionate graphic designer based in Baton Rouge, LA. With a degree in Art and two years of valuable field experience, I bring a unique blend of creativity and expertise to every project I undertake. My mission is to turn my clients' visions into visual masterpieces. I believe in the power of design to communicate, captivate, and inspire. Whether it's crafting a brand identity, designing eye-catching marketing materials, or creating stunning visuals, I am dedicated to bringing ideas to life through the lens of graphic design. I take pride in my ability to understand the essence of each project and translate it into a visual language that resonates. Every design is a thoughtful and intentional expression of the client's goals and aspirations. Let's collaborate and bring your vision to life! I'm here to transform ideas into impactful designs that leave a lasting impression.
